Janki Mandir

Janki Mandir is situated at Janankpuri in Nepal. We can visit here via train from Darbhanga, Jaynagar, Raxaul to Janakpur or via bus from Patna or by air. The speciality of this temple is it is the birth place of Lord Sita mata at her father's residence in devyuga. Being the princess of Janakpur she had married with the eldest son of raja Dasrath shri Ram and thereafter they went to the jungle along with ram's brother Lakshman for 14 years of meditation.
